Featured Project

Nuuky

A social app for introverts — designed and built solo, from concept to App Store.

Solo Project

A calm way to feel close to friends

Social connection without the noise.

I noticed my introverted friends drifting away — not because they didn't care, but because texting felt like too much. So I designed and built Nuuky: friends as glowing orbs showing mood and presence, silent nudges instead of messages, voice rooms with lo-fi music for quiet company. No feeds, no read receipts, no pressure.

React Native TypeScript Figma Reanimated Expo
Visit nuuky.app

Product & UX Design

Came up with the concept, designed every screen in Figma, created the brand identity, and iterated based on feedback from friends testing early builds. The orbit metaphor was the core design challenge — making presence feel warm, not creepy.

UI & Interactions

Built the entire interface in React Native with custom animated components: orbiting particles with spring physics, swipeable cards with haptic feedback, mood-driven color theming, and a lo-fi music player with crossfade transitions.

Shipping It

Took the project from an idea to a production app in the App Store. Handled everything: design system, component library, animations, the marketing website, and learning whatever I needed along the way — including leveraging AI tools to move fast.
